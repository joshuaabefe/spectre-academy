Frontend Project Presentation

Spectre Academy Admission Portal

A modern multi-step admission experience designed to help students complete applications smoothly across desktop and mobile devices.

Project Summary

Goals And Problem Solved

The portal replaces a rigid and confusing admission flow with a more structured, user-friendly interface.

  • Multi-step guided application form
  • Progress tracking and review flow
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Persistent local storage support
Design System

Brand Direction

The identity uses a shield-based logo, dark academic tones, and bright cyan highlights to make the product feel premium, modern, and memorable.

  • Rebrand from Westbrook Academy to Spectre Academy
  • Custom SVG logo and favicon
  • Updated interface colours and surfaces
  • Stronger visual hierarchy
